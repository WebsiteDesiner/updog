How to buy $UPDOG

Create a wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $UPDOG. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Visit uniswap

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $UPDOG token address into Uniswap, select $UPDOG, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch ETH for UPDOG

Switch ETH for $UPDOG. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
